About Sri Sowmya Keshava Temple

Nestled in the serene village of Madihalli, Hassan district, Karnataka, the Sri Sowmya Keshava Temple is a sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Keshava, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. This temple is renowned for its intricate South Indian architecture, peaceful ambiance, and religious significance. It serves as a spiritual haven for devotees and a destination for architectural enthusiasts.

Located at: Madihalli, Hassan, Karnataka 573216, this temple is easily accessible from the nearby towns of Hassan and surrounding areas. Whether you're visiting for worship, heritage exploration, or tranquility, the temple offers a divine experience.
